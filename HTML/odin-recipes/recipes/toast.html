Avocado Toast

Ingredients

  • 4 slices whole-grain bread
  • 1 avocado, halved and pitted
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 ½ teaspoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • ½ lemon, juiced
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ⅓ teaspoon onion powder
  • ⅔ teaspoon garlic powder

Directions

  1. Toast bread in a toaster or toaster oven.
  2. Scoop avocado into a bowl.
  3. Add parsley, olive oil, lemon juice, salt, pepper, onion powder, and garlic powder.
  4. Mash together using a potato masher.
  5. Spread avocado mixture into each piece of toast.
